ABOUT

I love engineering. I love learning. I love applying what I learn to create solutions. When it comes to what I love, I used to want to be the best - but in my experience nobody is the best alone. When it comes to what I love, I will be part of the best.

I’m a hardware engineer of many hats knee-deep in mixed-signal electronics design, novel materials, circuit boards in every form factor, and the practical details that make hardware work at every scale of production. Professionally, my work has included analog stacks for optoelectronic metrology, prototype debug, EMI/ESD robustness, aerospace reliability procedure, and rigorous analytical diligence the likes of which would make an actuary blush.
